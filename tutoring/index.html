Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 How are you qualified to tutor me?

I'm qualitied for three reasons:

✔ I have a BS in Mathematics and attended grad school for math for a couple years.

✔ I teach math at a K-8th school in Phoenix. I'm what's called a Math Interventionist. Basically I'm at expert in teaching kids that are behind in math.

✔ I've tutored lots of students before that did wonderfully on their homeworks and exams with my help.

🤔 Why don't you offer virtual sessions?

I don't offer virtual sessions as the video call medium does not lend itself towards quality interaction between student and teacher. A lot of the teaching methodology requires face-to-face to work well.

🤔 Why don't you have testimonials here?

I think testimonials are kinda flashy and cheap. Anyone can just make them up. I prefer to get referals from former students or let my first session validate my skills.

If you haven't been referred by a former student of mine, I'll give you 25% off your first session. Ask for the discount when you call/text me.

🤔 If I don't pass my class, will you really give my money back?

Yes. Assuming you've been attending a sessions with me at least once a week and I have evidence that you study or do homework at home. Lastly, I cannot offer this guarantee to students that have started with me less than a month before their class ends.
